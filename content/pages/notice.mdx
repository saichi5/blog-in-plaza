## 1. Real-time coauthoring in Markdown

Write documents as a team with Boost Note&apos;s realtime editing using a versatile Markdown editor. You can put diagrams with Charts.js, Mermaid, and PlantUML in documents to maximize visibility.

![](https://boostnote.io/static/images/initial/collaborativeeditor.png)



## 2. Document version history

Check revision history of a doc. You can easily roll back to one of the previous versions in one click.

![](https://boostnote.io/static/images/initial/revision.png)



## 3. Comments & Mentions

You can add comments to the whole document or specific parts of the document. If you need, you can even mention your specific team member to send a notification and get their attention.

![](https://boostnote.io/static/images/initial/comment.png)



## 4. Access control

You can choose among [three different access controls](https://intercom.help/boostnote-for-teams/en/articles/4354888-roles) for each one of your team members.

![](https://boostnote.io/static/images/initial/roles.png)



## 5. Share publicly

You can share your documents with your clients and outside partners really easily. Just use our public sharing feature! You can set a password and expiration date as well.

![](https://boostnote.io/static/images/initial/shareoptions.png)
